Typography
Rohr, Maria

General

The leading font for our brand is Montserrat. It is progressive and has a special personality representing the SoftwareONE brand.

It is used in online media, print publications, presentations and headlines of business stationery.

For the bodytext of business stationery (Outlook, Word, Excel) the font Calibri is used to maintan functionality.
